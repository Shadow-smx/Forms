Han Sooyoung | The Author

“She laughed at gods, rewrote destinies, and turned tragedy into ink. Her pen was rebellion, her story a weapon — for even lies can save a world when told by the right hand.”

✒️ Han Sooyoung — The Trickster Who Wrote the End

Han Sooyoung is a sharp-tongued, brilliant author — the original writer of the novel Kim Dokja once read. Cynical yet deeply loyal, she hides her pain behind sarcasm and laughter. Even after the world turned into her own fiction, she continued to write, not for fame, but to protect her friends and finish the tale they all lived through. She embodies defiance — rewriting destiny itself with every stroke of her pen.
