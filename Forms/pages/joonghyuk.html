Yoo Joonghyuk | The Protagonist

“Born to die and die to be reborn — the warrior who broke time itself. Through endless lifetimes he fought, not for glory, but to grasp the hope that always slipped through his bloodstained hands.”


⚔️ Yoo Joonghyuk — The Regressor Who Defied Fate

Yoo Joonghyuk is the original protagonist of Three Ways to Survive in a Ruined World. Cursed to repeat countless regressions, he relives the apocalypse over and over, seeking the perfect ending. Hardened by tragedy and solitude, he becomes a living weapon of purpose — yet beneath his cold exterior is a heart still capable of hope, loyalty, and pain. Through his endless struggle, he embodies humanity’s refusal to surrender to despair.
